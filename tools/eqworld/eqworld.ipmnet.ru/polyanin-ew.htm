Andrei Dmitrievich POLYANIN

Ph.D., D.Sc., Professor

Born: 1 November 1951

Education and Degrees:

  • M.Sc., 1974, Faculty of Mechanics and Mathematics of the Lomonosov Moscow State University; graduated with honours;
  • Ph.D., 1981, Institute for Problems in Mechanics of the Russian Academy of Sciences, Moscow;
  • D.Sc., 1987, Institute for Problems in Mechanics of the Russian Academy of Sciences, Moscow.

Current Professional Status:

  • Professor, Institute for Problems in Mechanics of the Russian Academy of Sciences
  • Professor, Department of Fundamental Sciences, Bauman Moscow State Technical University
  • Professor, Department of Applied Mathematics, National Research Nuclear University MEPhI

Membership:

  • Mathematics and Mechanics Expert Council, Higher Certification Committee, Ministry of Education and Science of the Russian Federation;
  • Russian National Committee on Theoretical and Applied Mechanics;
  • Editorial Board of the Journal Theoretical Foundations of Chemical Engineering;
  • Editorial Board of the Journal Mathematical Advances in Pure and Applied Sciences;
  • Editorial Board of the Journal Mathematical Modeling and Numerical Methods (in Russian);
  • Editorial Board of the Journal Vestnik Natsional'nogo Issledovatel'skogo Yadernogo Universiteta "MIFI" (in Russian);
  • Editorial Advisors of Research Signpost (India).

Editor:

  • Website EqWorld: The World of Mathematical Equations (http://eqworld.ipmnet.ru);
  • Website MechMath: The Mechanics and Applied Mathematics (http://mechmath.ipmnet.ru, in Russian);
  • Book series Differential and Integral Equations and Their Applications, Chapman & Hall/CRC Press, Boca Raton–London.

Honours, Awards and Prizes:

  • Chaplygin Prize of the Russian Academy of Sciences, 1991;
  • Award from the Ministry of Education of the Russian Federation, 2001;
  • Russian State Scientific Stipend to Outstanding Scientists, 1997–2003;
  • Listed in numerous biographical reference books and dictionaries in USA, UK and Russia

Areas of Expertise:

  • ordinary differential equations, partial differential equations,
  • mathematical physics, integral equations,
  • functional equations, delay partial differential equations,
  • engineering and applied mathematics, nonlinear mechanics,
  • heat and mass transfer, fluid mechanics,
  • physico-chemical hydrodynamics, chemical engineering sciences

Author:

  • More than 30 books in English, Russian, German, and Bulgarian including some best-selling handbooks;
  • more than 210 research papers and three patents.
