Useful Mathematical Web Sites

  • MathWorld: Wolfram Web Resource by Eric W. Weisstein, a subject gateway to mathematics
  • Automated e-print Archives of Articles (arXiv.org), ArXiv is an e-print service in the fields of physics, mathematics, non-linear science, and computer science (Cornell University & the National Science Foundation)
    • Exactly Solvable and Integrable Systems, recent papers
    • Mathematical Physics, recent papers
    • Analysis of PDEs, recent papers
  • S.O.S. Mathematics, a free resource for math review material from Algebra to Differential Equations
  • Wikipedia: The Free Encyclopedia – Mathematics
    • Ordinary Differential Equations
    • Partial Differential Equations
  • Mathematical Atlas by Dave Rusin, a gateway to modern mathematics
  • Scholarpedia, the free peer reviewed encyclopedia
  • PlanetMath.Org, Mathematics Encyclopedia
  • EDUCYPEDIA: The Educational Encyclopedia – Mathematics
  • Science Oxygen: Mathematics
  • EEVL: Internet Guide to Engineering, Mathematics and Computing, cross-search 20 databases in engineering, maths and computing
  • Mathematical Constants and Numbers, mathematical, historical and algorithmic aspects of some classical mathematical constants; easy and fast programs are also included

General Directories of Math Web Sites

  • Google: Mathematics Web Sites, a directory of more than 11,000 Mathematics websites ordered by type and mathematical subject
    • Differential Equations
    • Mathematical Physics
    • Calculator, performing mathematical functions such as logarithms, sines, cosines, factorials, and exponents
    • Google Scholar, provides a search of scholarly literature across many disciplines and sources, including theses, books, abstracts and articles
  • Yahoo: Mathematics Web Sites, a directory of mathematics websites ordered by type and mathematical subject
  • Math Forum: Internet Mathematics Library, a directory of mathematics websites ordered by the mathematical subject
  • Mathematical WWW Virtual Library (Florida State University), a directory of mathematics-related websites ordered by type and mathematical subject
  • UF Mathematics (University of Florida), a directory of mathematics-related websites ordered by type and mathematical subject
  • Internet Guide to Engineering, Mathematics and Computing, a directory of mathematics websites ordered by type and mathematical subject
  • Mathematics Web Sites (Pennsylvania State University), directory of mathematics websites ordered by type and mathematical subject
  • A Catalog of Mathematics Resources on the WWW and the Internet (University of Wisconsin Marathon County)
  • MathGuide (SUB Gottingen), a Internet-based subject-gateway to mathematics-related websites
  • Mathematics Archives (University of Tennessee), combined archive and directory of mathematics web sites, mailing lists, and teaching materials
  • Math-Net (International Mathematical Union), electronic information and communication system for mathematics

Useful Mathematical Links

  • Mathematics Genealogy Project, a large biographical database of mathematicians
  • Symmetry Links, Web Links to Resources on Symmetry, Group Theory, and Related Subjects in Mathematics and Physics
  • Symmetries and Integrability: Personal Web Pages, an extensive list of personal Web pages of individuals involved in symmetries and integrability of mathematical equations
  • International Research Centre ALGA: Advances in Lie Group Analysis
  • Green's Function Library, a collection of Green's functions and solutions for linear partial differential equations of the diffusion type
  • Chaos Hypertextbook: chaos, fractals, strange attractors, non-linear dynamics, and others
  • Chaos Gallery: attractor pictures, chaos animations, fractal photos, and others
  • Probability Web, a collection of probability resources on the World Wide Web; the pages are designed to be especially helpful to researchers and teachers
  • VassarStats: Web Site for Statistical Computation, most popular statistical tests to analyze their data, textbook "Concepts and Applications of Inferential Statistics", and others
  • Rice Virtual Lab in Statistics: an online statistics book, Java applets that demonstrate various statistical concepts, examples of real data with analyses, and statistics resources on the web
  • Virtual Polyhedra: Encyclopedia of Polyhedra (collection of thousands of virtual reality polyhedra)
  • Partial Differential Equations and Mathematical Biology, Professor Junping Shi
  • Science-Search.org, a scientific web directory
