Free Mathematical Books on the Internet

  • PlanetMath.Org - A virtual community which aims to help make mathematical knowledge more accessible
    • Books in electronic form
    • Lectures and lecture notes in electronic form
  • American Mathematical Society: links to mathematics books online and individual titles online
  • The Electronic Library of Mathematics, European Mathematical Society & FIZ Karlsruhe / Zentralblatt MATH
    • Mathematical Monographs and Lecture Notes
  • Mathematical Sciences Digital Library, online resources for both teachers and students of mathematics (Mathematical Association of America)
  • National Science Digital Library (NSDL): USA online library of resources for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics education and research
  • Directory.Google.Com: online mathematics books
  • Georgia Institute of Technology: online mathematics textbooks
  • Distributed Digital Library of Mathematical Monographs Collection (the Gottingen State and University Library in Germany, the University of Michigan, and Cornell University): An online math library packed with full-text versions of more than 2000 books and monographs from the 19th and early 20th centuries
  • Cornell Digital Library, The Cornell University Library: Historical Mathematics Monographs
  • Free Math Textbooks at Geocities, a list of links to useful mathematical textbooks
  • Online Mathematics Textbooks
  • FreeScience.info - Free Scientific Books
    • Mathematics
    • Physics
  • FreeTechBooks.com: Free computer science books and lecture notes
  • Samizdat Press is devoted to the free distribution of books, lecture notes and software
  • Several Calculus Textbooks
  • Foundations of Mathematics (Editor: Alexander Sakharov), more than 50 links into chapters to create a virtual textbook, with writings from mathematicians, computer scientists, and other experts
  • BookRags.com, Online Encyclopedia - Mathematics
  • HyperStat Online Textbook, an online statistics book with links to other statistics resources on the web
  • NIST/SEMATECH e-Handbook of Statistical Methods, measurement, selecting statistical models, and data analysis, using samples and in-depth case studies that emphasize engineering problems
  • Chaos Hypertextbook, chaos, fractals, strange attractors, non-linear dynamics, and others
