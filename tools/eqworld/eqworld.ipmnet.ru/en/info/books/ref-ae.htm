Books on Algebraic Equations

  • Birkhoff, G. and Mac Lane, S., A Survey of Modern Algebra, 5th Edition, Macmillan, New York, 1996.
  • Dehn, E., Algebraic Equations: An Introduction to the Theories of Lagrange and Galois, Dover Publ., 2004.
  • Garling, D. J. H., A Course in Galois Theory, Cambridge Univ. Press, 1989.
  • King, R. B., Beyond the Quartic Equation, Birkhauser, Boston, 1996.
  • Rotman, J., Galois Theory, Springer-Verlag, New York, 1998.
  • Shurman, J., Geometry of the Quintic, Wiley-Interscience, 1997.
  • Stewart, I., Galois Theory, Third Edition, Chapman & Hall/CRC, Boca Raton, 2003.
  • Weisstein, E. W., CRC Concise Encyclopedia of Mathematics, Second Edition, pp. 2443-2445, 2453-2456, Chapman & Hall/CRC, Boca Raton, 2002.
  • Zwillinger, D., CRC Standard Mathematical Tables and Formulae, 31st Edition, pp. 89-91, Chapman & Hall/CRC, Boca Raton, 2003.
