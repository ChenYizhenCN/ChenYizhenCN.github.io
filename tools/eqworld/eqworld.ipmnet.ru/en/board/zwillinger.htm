Daniel ZWILLINGER

Ph.D., Professor

Born: 1957

Highlights:

Applied mathematician experienced in large and small companies, government labs, consulting, and academia. Twenty years successful experience in applying mathematical modeling and analysis to problems in the engineering and physical sciences.

  • College professor for 4 years at RPI.
  • Technical expertise in the engineering and physical sciences.
  • Extensive experience in algorithm design/implementation, modeling, simulation, and numerical methods.
  • Computer skills: UNIX, MATLAB, Mathematica, Perl, Python, SQL, C++.
  • Qualified 6-sigma specialist

Education and Degree:

  • SB in Mathematics, 1978, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, USA;
  • PhD in Applied Mathematics, 1983, California Institute of Technology, USA

Current Professional Status:

  • Senior Principal Engineer, Raytheon Company, Lexington, MA, USA

Areas of Expertise:

  • mathematical modeling and algorithm design;
  • systems engineering
