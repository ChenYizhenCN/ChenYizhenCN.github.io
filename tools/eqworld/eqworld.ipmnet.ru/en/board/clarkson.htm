Peter A. CLARKSON

Ph.D., Professor

Born: 9th May 1957

Education and Degree:

  • B.A., July 1979, Mathematics, University of Oxford, UK;
  • D.Phil., July 1983, Mathematics, University of Oxford, UK

Current Professional Status:

  • Professor of Mathematics, Institute of Mathematics, Statistics and Actuarial Science, University of Kent, Canterbury, UK

Editorial Board Membership:

  • European Journal of Applied Mathematics
  • Journal of Nonlinear Mathematical Physics
  • Quarterly Journal of Mechanics and Applied Mathematics
  • Studies in Applied Mathematics
  • EqWorld: The World of Mathematical Equations

Areas of Expertise:

  • soliton theory, the Painlevé equations, Painlevé analysis;
  • classical, direct, and nonclassical symmetry reductions and exact solutions of nonlinear partial differential equations;
  • asymptotics, Bäcklund transformations, connection formulae and exact solutions for nonlinear ordinary differential and difference equations;
  • symbolic manipulation programmes for symmetry analysis;
  • reductions of the self-dual Yang-Mills equations

Author:

  • 5 books;
  • more than 100 research papers
