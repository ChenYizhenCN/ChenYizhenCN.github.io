Alexei Ivanovich ZHUROV

Ph.D., Associate Professor

Born: 9 March 1967, Moscow Region, Russia

Education:

  • 1984–1990, Faculty of Airphysics and Space Research, Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology, Dolgoprudny, Russia; graduated with honours;
  • 1990–1994, Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology, Dolgoprudny, Russia; Ph.D. course.

Degrees:

  • 1990, M.Sc., Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology, Dolgoprudny, Russia;
  • 1995, Ph.D., Institute for Problems in Mechanics, Russian Academy of Sciences, Moscow, Russia.

Current Professional Status:

  • Senior Researcher, Institute for Problems in Mechanics, Russian Academy of Sciences, Moscow, Russia;
  • Associate Professor, Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology, Dolgoprudny, Russia;
  • Research Officer, Cardiff University, Cardiff, UK.

Membership:

  • Editorial Board of the website EqWorld: The World of Mathematical Equations (http://eqworld.ipmnet.ru).
  • European Society of Biomechanics.
  • MediTech: The Research Network for Medical Engineering.
  • Medical Devices Faraday Partnership Network.

Awards:

  • 1997–2000, Russian State Scientific Stipend to Young Scientists;
  • 2000–2003, Russian State Scientific Stipend to Outstanding Scientists.

Areas of Expertise:

  • nonlinear differential equations, exact solutions,
  • theory of heat and mass transfer and chemical hydrodynamics,
  • soft-tissue and bone biomechanics,
  • geometric morphometrics,
  • dynamical systems,
  • computer algebra.

Author:

  • 3 books,
  • over 70 research articles.
