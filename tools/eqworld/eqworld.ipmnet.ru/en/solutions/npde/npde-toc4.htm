4. Other Second-Order Partial Differential Equations

4.1. Equations of Transonic Gas Flow

  1. awxwxx + wyy = 0.    Equation of steady transonic gas flow.
  2. wyy + ay−1wy + bwxwxx = 0.    Equation of steady transonic gas flow.

4.2. Monge-Ampere Equations

  1. (wxy)2 − wxxwyy = 0.    Homogeneous Monge-Ampere equation.
  2. (wxy)2 − wxxwyy = A.    Nonhomogeneous Monge-Ampere equation.
  3. (wxy)2 − wxxwyy = F(x, y).    Nonhomogeneous Monge-Ampere equation.
