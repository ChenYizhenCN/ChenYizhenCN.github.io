For Authors > Write/Publish Book

You want to write and publish a book?

Send your projects to Professor Andrei D. Polyanin, Editor of the series Differential and Integral Equations and Their Applications with Chapman & Hall/CRC Press

First, you should send the following to eqworld@ipmnet.ru:
  • an abstract or foreword (at least one full page),
  • a detailed table of contents (at least three pages),
  • information about the authors (as you wish).

Monographs should meet these requirements:

  • cover a wide range of issues,
  • present not only authors' results but also suitable results by other scientists in this and related areas,
  • use simple and clear language (without excessive use of terminology),
  • go from simple to complex (for the reader's easier understanding),
  • optionally include suitable supplements.
  

The EqWorld website presents extensive information on solutions to various classes of ordinary differential equations, partial differential equations, integral equations, functional equations, and other mathematical equations.
